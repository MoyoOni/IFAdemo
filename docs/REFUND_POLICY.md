# Payment Refund Policy

## Overview

At Ilé Àṣẹ, we understand that sometimes plans change. Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both service providers and clients.

---

## Refund Eligibility

You may request a refund in the following situations:

### ✅ 100% Refund

You will receive a **full refund** (100% of payment) if:

1. **Service Provider Cancels** - The Babalawo or service provider cancels your appointment or service
2. **Service Not Completed** - The scheduled service was not provided or completed
3. **Dispute Resolution** - A dispute is resolved in your favor by our admin team
4. **Technical Issues** - Platform technical issues prevented service delivery

### ⚠️ 50% Refund

You will receive a **partial refund** (50% of payment) if:

1. **You Cancel After Booking** - You decide to cancel after the booking is confirmed
2. **Other Reasons** - Any other cancellation reason not listed above

---

## How to Request a Refund

### For Appointments/Bookings

1. Go to your **Bookings** page
2. Find the appointment you want to cancel
3. Click **"Cancel & Request Refund"**
4. Select your cancellation reason
5. Add any additional notes (optional)
6. Click **"Request Refund"**

### For Marketplace Orders

1. Go to **My Orders**
2. Find the order you want to refund
3. Click **"Request Refund"**
4. Select your cancellation reason
5. Submit your request

### For Admin-Resolved Disputes

If you have a dispute, contact our support team. Admins can issue refunds directly through the dispute resolution system.

---

## Refund Processing Time

- **Wallet Refunds**: Instant (funds returned to your Ilé Àṣẹ wallet)
- **Original Payment Method**: 5-10 business days
  - Paystack: 5-7 business days
  - Flutterwave: 7-10 business days

**Note**: Refund processing times depend on your bank or payment provider.

---

## Refund Policy Details

### Cancellation Deadlines

- **24+ hours before appointment**: Eligible for refund based on reason
- **Less than 24 hours**: Subject to provider's cancellation policy
- **No-show**: No refund

### Partial Services

If a service is partially completed, refunds are calculated based on:
- Percentage of service completed
- Time spent by provider
- Admin discretion for disputes

### Marketplace Products

- **Digital Products**: No refunds after download/access
- **Physical Products**: Refund available before shipping
- **Services**: Follow standard refund policy

---

## Exceptions

Refunds may **NOT** be available for:

1. **Services Already Completed** - If the service was fully delivered
2. **Digital Content Accessed** - Once digital products are downloaded or accessed
3. **Fraudulent Requests** - Abuse of refund system may result in account suspension
4. **Expired Bookings** - Refund requests made more than 30 days after service date

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does a refund take?

Wallet refunds are instant. Refunds to your original payment method take 5-10 business days depending on your bank.

### Can I get a refund if I cancel 1 hour before my appointment?

Yes, but you'll receive a 50% refund for user-initiated cancellations.

### What if the Babalawo cancels?

You'll receive a 100% refund if the service provider cancels.

### Can I get a refund for a service I didn't like?

Refunds are not based on satisfaction. However, if the service was not completed as agreed, you may be eligible for a refund through our dispute resolution process.

### Where does my refund go?

Refunds are returned to your Ilé Àṣẹ wallet by default. You can then withdraw to your bank account or use for future bookings.

### Can I request a partial refund?

Partial refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis through our dispute resolution system. Contact support for assistance.
