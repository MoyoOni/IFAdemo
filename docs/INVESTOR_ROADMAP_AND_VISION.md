## 2. Future Feature Horizons (Post-MVP)

### Horizon 1: The "Wisdom Engine" (AI & Personalization)
*Goal: Deepen engagement through personalized, daily spiritual touchpoints.*

*   **Àṣẹ AI Assistant ("Orunmila's Scribe"):**
    *   **For Users:** A conversational interface to ask cultural questions (e.g., "What is the significance of kola nuts?"). It cites verified sources from the platform's Academy.
    *   **For Priests:** An assistant to help draft detailed reading summaries, schedule follow-ups, and organize client history (reducing admin time by 70%).
*   **Daily Energy Forecast (Biorythms + Odu):**
    *   A personalized "Daily Reading" dashboard that combines the user's *Orisha* alignment with the day's verify Ifá calendar energy.
    *   Push notifications: "Today is a day for **Coolness (Oshe)**. Wear white and avoid conflict."

### Horizon 2: The "Global Village" (Immersion & Live Interaction)
*Goal: Replicate the feeling of physical presence digitally.*

*   **Virtual Temple Sanctuaries (Metaverse Lite):**
    *   3D/Immersive views of Verified Temples. Users can "walk in," light a virtual candle, or leave an offering.
    *   **Live Ritual Streaming:** Ticketed access to major festivals (e.g., Osun Osogbo) streamed in 4K from Nigeria/Brazil to the diaspora.
*   **The Council of Elders (DAO):**
    *   A decentralized governance body for verification. High-ranking priests vote on distinct cultural disputes or verify new priests, ensuring the platform remains community-owned and authentic.

### Horizon 3: The "Sacred Economy" (Fintech & Commerce)
*Goal: Professionalize and secure the spiritual economy.*

*   **"Cowrie" Token / Credits System:**
    *   Platform-specific credits for micro-transactions (tipping a drummer, pay-per-question, unlocking a single lesson).
*   **Provenance Blockchain:**
    *   Digital Authenticity Certificates for expensive artifacts (e.g., hand-carved Ikin). Buyers can scan a QR code to see the wood source, the carver, and the blessing priest.
*   **Subscription Boxes ("The Monthly Calabash"):**
    *   Recurring revenue model. Users receive a curated box of ingredients (e.g., soaps, candles, oils) from verified vendors every month tailored to their needs.
